Questions, answered

Does my audio ever leave my phone?
No. Transcription runs entirely on-device with the HushBook Engine. There's no cloud, no account, and the app works fully offline after a one-time model download.
Where do the synced words come from?
HushBook generates them from your audio — there's no lyrics database. The first pass on a long book takes a while because it's doing real speech-to-text on your phone; after that, it's instant every time you open the book.
What files can I import?
MP3, M4A, M4B, AAC, WAV, FLAC, OGG/OPUS and more, one file per import. M4B chapter markers are detected automatically, and cover art and metadata are pulled in for you.
Will it drain my battery or heat my phone?
The Engine is thermal-aware — it caps its worker threads and idles between chunks to stay cool on long transcriptions, and resumes automatically if it's ever interrupted.
How much does it cost?
The core read-along experience and the public-domain library are free. There's an optional Pro upgrade for advanced analytics — going Pro helps support HushBook's ongoing development.
Can I read in the dark?
Yes — a night mode swaps the blurred cover backdrop for a solid dark canvas, and the Vision profile adds high-contrast themes with very large captions.
