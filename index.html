Questions

Everything you might be wondering.

Still curious? Email hello@hushbook.app and we'll answer.

Is HushBook out yet?+

Not yet — HushBook is still in active development. It's an indie project, and iOS and Android will launch together. Join the waitlist and we'll let you know the moment it's ready.

Does my audio ever leave my phone?+

No. Transcription runs entirely on-device with the HushBook Engine — there's no cloud upload, no account, and no sign-in. Your audiobooks and your listening data stay on your phone. Once a model is downloaded, the whole thing works fully offline.

What audio files can I import?+

MP3, M4B, M4A and most common audio formats, one file per import. M4B chapter markers are detected automatically, and you can add more chapters to a book later. HushBook reads the duration and metadata, then starts transcribing in the background.

Where do the synced lyrics come from?+

HushBook generates them itself. There's no lyrics database — the HushBook Engine runs on your imported audio to produce word-level timings, then highlights each word as the narrator speaks it. The first transcription of a long book takes a while; after that it's instant every time you open it.

How much does it cost?+

The core read-along experience is free, and so is the built-in library of public-domain LibriVox and Internet Archive audiobooks. Some advanced analytics are planned as an optional upgrade, but reading along with your own books costs nothing.

Who is it built for?+

Anyone who reads with their ears — and especially people served by our Vision, Dyslexia and Comprehension accessibility profiles. Pick one once and fonts, caption sizes and colours adapt across the entire app: Atkinson Hyperlegible and large captions for low vision, OpenDyslexic with reading overlays for dyslexia and ADHD, and focused comprehension tools for everyone else.
